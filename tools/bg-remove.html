💡 How the AI Background Remover Works

This tool uses the RMBG-1.4 AI segmentation model, running entirely in your browser via WebAssembly. Your images never leave your device — complete privacy guaranteed.

  • First use: The AI model (~60MB) is downloaded once and cached in your browser. Subsequent uses are instant.
  • Best results: Works best on photos with clear subject-background contrast (portraits, product photos, animals).
  • Transparent PNG: Choose PNG output to preserve transparency for use in design tools.
  • Custom background: Replace the removed background with any solid color — perfect for e-commerce product photos.
