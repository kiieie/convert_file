💡 SVG to Raster Image Conversion

SVG is a vector format, meaning it is mathematically defined and can scale infinitely without pixelation. Converting it to PNG or JPG converts it into a raster image composed of pixels.

  • High Resolution Export: You can set very large widths and heights (e.g. 2000px or 4000px) to export high-definition raster graphics from your SVG.
  • Transparency Support: PNG format supports transparency, retaining the clear background of your SVG. JPG format does not support transparency and renders a white background instead.
  • Completely Local: The conversion uses HTML5 Canvas rendering in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server.
