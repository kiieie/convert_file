💡 How to Pick & Extract Colors

All extraction runs in your browser via HTML5 Canvas API. Your pictures are never uploaded to any server.

  • Dominant Palette: The algorithm divides the image into a grid, clusters similar pixels, and shows the top 6 most frequent colors automatically.
  • Pixel Picker: Hover and click on the loaded image canvas to select any precise pixel color. The details panel updates in real-time.
  • Copy Codes: Click "Copy HEX Code" or click on any color circle from the dominant palette to load it into the picker and copy its value.
