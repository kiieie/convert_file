💡 WebP Converter Guide

All conversions run entirely in your browser — your files are never uploaded to a server. WebP is Google's modern image format offering superior compression compared to JPEG and PNG.

  • To WebP: Upload any JPG, PNG, BMP, or GIF and click To WebP. Typical savings: 25–35% vs JPG, up to 50% vs PNG.
  • From WebP: Upload a WebP file and convert it to JPG (maximum compatibility) or PNG (preserves transparency).
  • Quality slider: Applies to WebP and JPG outputs (range 50–100, default 85). PNG is always lossless and ignores the slider.
  • Transparency: WebP and PNG support transparent backgrounds. Converting to JPG fills transparent areas with the selected background color (default white).
  • GIF / BMP: Only the first frame is converted (static image). Use a dedicated GIF tool for animated GIFs.
