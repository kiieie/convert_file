💡 Watermarking Best Practices

Watermarking occurs completely in-browser. Your image stays private.

  • Logo Watermark: Use transparent PNG files for your logo to avoid ugly white background boxes overlaying your photos.
  • Tiled Position: Ideal for proofing. Repeated watermarks diagonally across the page make it very difficult for unauthorized crops.
  • Opacity Balance: 30% to 50% opacity is recommended. It is visible enough to deter theft but light enough not to distract from the main subject.
