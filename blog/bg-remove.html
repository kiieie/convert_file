1. What is Background Removal?

Background removal separates the foreground subject (e.g., a person, product, or logo) from its background. The resulting image is saved in a PNG container, which supports alpha channels (transparency), letting you seamlessly layer the subject onto new layouts.

4. Tips for Perfect AI Cutouts

The quality of the cutout depends heavily on the source image. To get the best results, try to use photos with:

  • High Contrast: A clear distinction between the color of the foreground subject and the background.
