1. What is WebP and Why Use It?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google, designed specifically for web performance. It combines the compression strengths of JPEG and the transparency features of PNG into a much smaller container.

  • JPEG Comparison: WebP lossless images are 25% to 34% smaller than comparable JPEGs.
  • PNG Comparison: WebP supports alpha channel transparency while being over 50% smaller than comparable PNGs.
  • GIF Comparison: WebP supports animation structures, offering massive file size reduction compared to traditional animated GIFs.

By replacing JPEGs with WebPs, you can easily reduce a page's total weight from 10MB to less than 3MB, significantly speeding up loading times.

2. WebP and Google SEO Benefits

Google uses **Core Web Vitals** as ranking factors to evaluate user experience. When you run a performance test on Google PageSpeed Insights, one of the most common recommendations is to **"Serve images in next-gen formats (WebP/AVIF)"**. Adopting WebP is one of the easiest ways to improve your site's SEO ranking.
