Privacy Policy

We transparently outline our policy of never uploading user files to servers, along with our use of third-party personalized advertising cookies.

1. Guarantee of No Server File Uploads

Our service does not require registration or any personally identifiable information. In particular, all image and video files dropped or selected by the user for processing are never transmitted to a remote server; all encoding and decoding are processed solely within the user's web browser.

2. Automatically Logged Data

For infrastructure monitoring and performance analysis, non-identifying device information such as cookie data, operating system type, and screen resolution may be automatically collected and logged.

3. Third-Party Partner Advertising Network Disclosure

To cover operation and maintenance costs, this free service may display banner ads served through third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdSense). These advertising partners use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Users who wish to opt out of personalized advertising can do so at any time by visiting the Google Ads Settings page.

4. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices or want to submit bug reports, please email us through our official support channel.
