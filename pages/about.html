About ConvertFile

Learn about the background and philosophy behind our free, browser-only web image tools that require no installation or registration.

⚡ 100% Client-Side Conversion

ConvertFile was developed to resolve the privacy concerns and upload latency associated with traditional server-based processing websites. All images and data you load are processed and downloaded directly within your browser session using the Canvas API and WebAssembly. We guarantee permanent local processing: no image files are ever uploaded or sent to our servers.

🎯 Sustainable Operations

By leveraging a fully static hosting environment (Cloudflare Pages) with zero bandwidth and server maintenance costs, we keep our operating expenses close to $0. Consequently, we do not require registration and pledge to keep our tools completely free, supported only by non-intrusive advertisements with no hidden fees.

🛡️ Security & Privacy Compliance

We integrate static templates and metadata removal modules that comply with GDPR guidelines and third-party advertising partners such as Google AdSense, contributing to a secure and transparent web ecosystem.
